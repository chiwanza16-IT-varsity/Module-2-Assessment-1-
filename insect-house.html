INSECT HOUSE

Welcome to the Insect House!

Join us for a day filled with wonder and excitement as we delve into the fascinating world of insects. Our daily program promises both education and entertainment for all ages.

Today's program:

  • 9:00 am: A Bug's Life - Experience the enchanting tale of tiny creatures overcoming big challenges in this captivating play.
  • 11:00 am: Penny and her Penguins - Join Penny on her adventurous journey with her penguin friends, where you'll learn valuable lessons about friendship and teamwork.
  • 2:00 pm: Big Cats of Africa - Immerse yourself in the majestic world of Africa's big cats through a breathtaking documentary that will leave you in awe.

Where to Find Us:

You can locate the Insect House to the west of the main entrance. Follow the signs or ask our friendly staff for directions.

Come and join us for a day of discovery and fun at the Insect House!
