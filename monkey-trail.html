MONKEY TRAIL

Welcome to the Monkey Trail

Prepare to swing into action on an adventure through the treetops of the Monkey Trail, where mischievous monkeys and other jungle inhabitants await your arrival. Get ready for a day packed with thrills and discoveries in this lively jungle setting.

Today's program:

  • 9:00 am: Breakfast with the Monkeys - Start your day by observing the playful antics of our resident monkeys as they enjoy their morning meal.
  • 11:00 am: Monkey Business - Join us for a lively presentation where you'll learn about the fascinating behaviors and social dynamics of these intelligent primates.
  • 2:00 pm: Jungle Jamboree - Dance to the rhythm of the jungle in this interactive musical performance featuring traditional music and dance from monkey habitats around the world.

Where to Find Us:

The Monkey Trail winds its way through the dense foliage of our park, offering a thrilling canopy experience for adventurers of all ages. Follow the trail markers and listen for the sounds of chattering monkeys to guide you on your journey.

Don't miss out on the excitement of the Monkey Trail!
