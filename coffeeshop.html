WILD THINGS COFFEE SHOP

Step into a world of flavor and adventure at Wild Things Coffee Shop, where every cup tells a tale of bold exploration and untamed spirit.

Escape the ordinary and embark on a sensory journey with our handcrafted coffee blends sourced from the wildest corners of the globe. From the rich earthiness of South American beans to the vibrant notes of African roasts, our menu celebrates the diversity of coffee culture while honoring the wild origins of each bean.

Today's Brews:

  • Safari Sunrise: Awaken your senses with our signature espresso blend, boasting hints of cocoa and citrus.
  • Jungle Java: Dive into a lush paradise of flavor with our medium-bodied Ethiopian pour-over, featuring notes of wild berries and floral undertones.
  • Explorer's Elixir: Indulge in a velvety-smooth latte infused with Madagascar vanilla and a touch of cinnamon spice.

Pair your coffee adventure with a delectable assortment of pastries and treats, freshly prepared to tantalize your taste buds and fuel your wild spirit.

Where to Find Us:

Located just steps away from the zoo entrance, Wild Things Coffee Shop invites you to savor the flavors of the wild in a cozy and inviting atmosphere. Whether you're beginning your day of exploration or seeking a midday pick-me-up, our friendly baristas are here to guide you on your coffee quest.

Join us at Wild Things Coffee Shop and let your taste buds roam free!
