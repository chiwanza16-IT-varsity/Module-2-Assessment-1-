MOS - PIZZA

Welcome to Mos Pizza, your delicious dining destination conveniently located within the zoo grounds!

After a day of exploring the wonders of wildlife, treat yourself to a mouthwatering selection of pizzas crafted with fresh ingredients and bursting with flavor. Whether you're craving a classic cheese pizza or adventurous toppings, we have something to satisfy every appetite.

Today's Specials:

  • Zookeeper's Choice: Our signature pizza topped with a savory blend of pepperoni, mushrooms, and bell peppers.
  • Jungle Feast: Dive into a tantalizing combination of grilled chicken, pineapple, and barbecue sauce.
  • Vegetarian Delight: Indulge in a garden-fresh pizza loaded with tomatoes, spinach, onions, and feta cheese.

Pair your pizza with a refreshing beverage or enjoy one of our delectable sides, such as garlic knots or crispy fries, for the ultimate dining experience.

Where to Find Us:

Mos Pizza is conveniently located near the heart of the zoo, making it the perfect spot to refuel and recharge during your visit. Look for our vibrant pizza cart nestled among the scenic surroundings, and follow the irresistible aroma of freshly baked crust.

Join us at Mos Pizza for a slice of happiness amidst the excitement of the zoo!
